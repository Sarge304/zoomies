Zoomies — Privacy Policy

Effective: April 1, 2026

Zoomies is a mobile app designed to entertain cats by displaying animated laser dots and visual stimuli on the screen.

Information We Collect

Zoomies does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information, location data, device identifiers, or usage analytics. The app operates entirely on-device and does not connect to the internet.

Camera

Zoomies may request access to your device camera for future features. If enabled, camera data is processed on-device only and is never transmitted, stored externally, or shared with any third party.

Children

Zoomies is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.

Third Parties

Zoomies contains no advertising, no analytics, and no third-party services. There are no third parties with any access to your data.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised effective date.
